Eastern NC Natural Disaster Food Relief

In Eastern North Carolina, hurricanes, tropical storms, and severe flooding are often catastrophic, recurring threats. These counties face immense challenges, especially in securing food security for those affected. Donating money to the Food Bank of Central & Eastern North Carolina in the wake of such calamities is not just a gesture of goodwill; it's a lifeline.

The map below highlights Eastern NC counties greatly affected by severe storms and food insecurity between 2009 and 2018. Our team at the Food Bank oversees a network of food pantries across Eastern NC and can assist in facilitating donations to support them.

We kindly encourage you to consider supporting the counties that have shown the greatest need, and we hope that this map aids you in directing your support.
